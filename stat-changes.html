Stat Changes ... Because We Can do BETTER!
Posted on July 17th



Stat Changes

Over the course of this game being out, for almost a year when this website is released, plants and zombies have been changed and updated in order to save space while coding them or to balance the cards: meaning to change the card such that it is not overpowered, but on the other hand, it is still playable by all. As of now, we have noticed many crippling problems with some of the cards sometimes that they do not really have the space and time on the battlefield to show how powerful these cards are. As a result, we are making these tiny edits to make the game a little bit more fair and manageable. *** FYI (a,h) means a is for attack, and h is for health.


Plant Classes


The Guardian Class

Poppin' Poppies; a flower which can make Lil' Buddies [(0,1) has team-up] here and next door (cost 6): Lil' Buddies can also heal your hero for 2 for each one present (maximum of 6 health can be restored to the Plant Hero's Health).

Wall-Nut Bowling: a trick that can make a Wallnut [(0,6); has Team-Up] on each Ground lane and attack for 6 damage in those lanes (cost 9): Sun cost is now 9 suns and not 10 suns.


The Kabloom Class

Sergant Strongberry: a berry that does 2 additional damage to a zombie which a berry has already hurt (cost 4): Now tankier [went from a (4,3) to a (4,4)].

Mushroom Grotto: an enviroment that spawns a Puff-Shroom [(1,1); has Team-Up] anywhere on the battlefield when a plant is played on the enviroment (cost 3): sun cost increased from 2 suns to 3 suns.

Kernel Corn: a corn that does 4 damage to each zombie on the battlefield (cost 8): It is now buffed from a (5,5) to a (6,6).


Mega-Grow Class

Party Thyme: a leafy plant that gives you a card from your deck when a plant does a bonus attack (cost 2): Has better attack but worse health. [went from a (1,5) to a (2,2)]


Smarty Class

Sow Magic Beans: a bean trick that shuffles 4 Magic Beanstalks [(4,4); cost 1; when played: draw a card] into your deck and draw a card after that (cost 2): This now also draws a card. (did not before)

Snapdragon: a dragon/flower that whenever he attacks regardless of attack, he does 3 damage to Zombies next door (cost 4): Now stronger. [went from a (3,1) to a (3,2)]

Brainana: a banana that is amphibious and makes the Zombie Hero lose brains; unable to play any tricks when their turn comes (cost 6): Now bigger. [from (3,3) to (4,3)]

Winter Melon: a fruit that does 4 damage, regardless of its attack, to Zombies next door. When it hurts a zombie, freeze the zombie. (cost 6): Now even more better! (cost is reduced from 7 to 6) and [now it went from (4,4) to a (4,5)]

Great Zucchini: a squash that transforms all zombies into (1,1) regular zombies (cost 9): Now stronger. [went from a (6,6) to a (7,7)]


Solar Class

Briar Rose: a flower plant that can kill a zombie that has damaged any flower on the battlefield (cost 4): Now cheaper. (cost sun was reduced from 6 to 4)

Twin Sunflower: a flower plant that can give you 2 suns on the battlefield at the start of every turn (cost 2): Now cheaper, but fragile. (cost sun was reduced from 3 to 2) and [stats are (0,1) as compared to (0,2)

Chomper: a flytrap plant that kills a zombie with (x less than or equal to 3,0) in the lane that the Chomper is played on (cost 4): Now tankier. [went from a (2,1) to a (2,2)]

Three-Headed Chomper: a flytrap plant that kills all zombies on the lane it is on, and the lanes adjacent to it at the end of every turn. (cost 6): Now tankier. [went from a (6,5) to a (6,6)]


Zombie Classes


Beastly Class

Deep Sea Gargantuar: a monster/gargantuar that is amphibious (cost 6): Now stronger. [went from a (6,6) to a (7,7)]

Nurse Gargantuar: a professional gargantuar that heals the Zombie Hero for how much damage he does to other plants or the Plant Hero (cost 7): Now stronger. [ went from a (4,6) to a (6,6)]

Octo Zombie: a pet zombie that is amphibious and can attack again when it destroys a plant. When destroyed, put this zombie back in your hand (cost 8): Now different... (can play the same Octo Zombie again and again; used to only play the same Octo Zombie only twice)


Brainy Class

Portal Technician: a mustache science zombie that becomes a random zombie when destroyed (cost 5): Now cheaper. [sun cost went from 6 brains to 5 brains]


Crazy Class

Aerobics Instructor: a dancing zombie that gives all dancing zombies (+2,0) at the start of each turn (cost 2): Now stronger. [went from a (1,3) to a (2,3)]

Discotron 3000: a dancing science zombie that hides in a gravestone until Zombies can play tricks and when revealed: it can make a Disco Zombie (3,1) which can make a (1,1) Backup Dancer (cost 6): Now has gravestone. (did not have this trait before)

Gargantuars' Feast: a foodlike gargantuar trick which can make 3 random Gargantuars in random lanes (cost 11): Now cheaper. [went from 12 suns to 11 suns]


Hearty Class

Team Mascot: a sports imp zombie that gives all sports zombies (+1,+1) at the start of each turn (cost 3); Now tankier. [went from a (1,3) to a (1,4)]

Zombie Coach: a mustache sports zombie that makes all sports zombies on the battlefield to not get hurt for that turns (cost 4): Now stronger. [went from a (2,3) to a (3,3)]

All-Star Zombie: a sports zombie that can reduce damage taken by 1, and can attack again if it destroys a plant (cost 5); Now tankier. [went from a (4,5) to a (4,6)]

Zombie King: a mustache history zombie that turns a random zombie into a Knight of the Living Dead [(5,5) Reduces incoming damage by 2, (cost 7)] at the end of every turn (cost 4): Now it can attack! [went from a (0,5) to a (2,5)]


Sneaky Class

No changes here!!!

Oh, A New Plant Hero?

Beta Carrotina is a special hero (plant on the left) which has appeared with the recent Galactic Gardens Update. She specifically looks like a carrot, and her name is a pun on the word carrot as well. She is a space hero, similar to how Citron, another hero, is portrayed as well. Coincidently, they both have the same classes too: having the Guardian Class and the Smarty Class.

Amazing! A New Zombie Hero!

Huge Giganticus is a special hero (zombie on the right) which has appeared with the recent Galactic Gardens Update. According to his title, Huge Giganticus should be a very big person, but ironically he is pretty small: an imp (small zombie) on a hovering platform. He shares the same classes as Super Brainz, another hero already in the game: The Brainy Class and the Sneaky Class.
